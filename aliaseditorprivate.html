Alias editor

Product design, UX design, UI design, Copywriting, Illustration

Creating new and adding suggested aliases in the most intuitive way came center to this design itteration of the Alias editor. Inspiration and guidence was taken from messaging and chat interfaces. The tool was able to generate new suggestions as the user contibuted more to it, giving a sense of partnership and teamwork with this AI.

The onboarding to the tool was added to the design to help a user uderstand the principles of the job at hand. This was to allow user with varying skill levels use the tool effectively. Illustrations were used here to add delight, and to pursuade someone to go on the onboarding journey, a commonly skipped task.

Along with the onboarding, a tour was designed to show the user the new tool and its unique aspects.
