Daughter List Process Info

So are humans the future of typography? Taking inspiration from processing and generative art, I combined this systematic aesthetic with a more fluid, expressive one. Curves of the human body meet structured grids. With M,o,T,y,and f revealed as the figures dance and move, there are undertones of the necessity of breaking the system, the rules and having fun for things to work.
