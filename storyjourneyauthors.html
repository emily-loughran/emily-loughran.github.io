A d r i a n T a h e n y

Adrian Taheny has appeared in many notable theatre productions, including Druid’s Threepenny Opera. He is retired from the Irish Financial Services industry and now pursues his passion for writing and photography. He had two stories published in Storytellers, New Writing from Ireland and two further stories in Sins, New Irish Crime Stories in Oct 2018. He is in the process of completing his first novel.
