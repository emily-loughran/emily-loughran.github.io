Services, Illustration, Copywriting

Client, Sweepr

Year, 2020

An article written and illustrated for Sweepr to communicate the value of research within the design team and their processes within design in the company, and to help other illustrators find a way to bring new lease of life to their illustation system design. I chose to write on the topic from my sudden introduction to the dicipline, after finding that the industry competitiors illustration design systems lacked original though at times, and looked like many other styles I had seen in other tech companies. The illustrations concept is seeing clarity or "an answer" through looking at visual research, putting things together and taking things away to isolate aspects. The style is done in the Sweepr house style, designed and developed by myself, along side my collegues in the UX team.
